Film Director

Louis Leterrier

Louis Leterrier is a French film director and producer, born on June 17, 1973, in Paris, France. He is best known for directing action and adventure movies. Some of his popular films include The Transporter (2002), The Incredible Hulk (2008), and Now You See Me (2013). Leterrier is known for making fast-paced, visually exciting movies, especially in the action and thriller genres. He has also worked on television and helped produce several projects in Hollywood.

Corey Yuen

Corey Yuen (1951–2022) was a Hong Kong film director, action choreographer, and actor. He was famous for creating exciting martial arts and action scenes in movies. He worked on many well-known films such as The Transporter (2002), DOA: Dead or Alive (2006), and The Expendables (2010), where he helped design the fight choreography. Corey Yuen played an important role in bringing Hong Kong–style action and martial arts to international movies.
