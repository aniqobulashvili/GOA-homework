Film Stars

Jason Statham] Shu Qi] Matt Schulze


Jason Statham

Jason Statham plays the main character, Frank Martin, in the movie The Transporter. Frank Martin is a professional driver who transports packages for criminals without asking questions. He is very skilled at driving and fighting, and he follows strict rules in his job. However, in the movie he breaks his rules, which leads to dangerous situations and many action scenes





Shu Qi

Shu Qi plays Lai Kwai in The Transporter. Lai Kwai is a young woman who is being illegally transported by criminals. She is brave and clever, and she asks Frank Martin for help. During the story, she becomes an important character who helps reveal the criminal operation.





Mat Schulze

Matt Schulze plays Wall Street in The Transporter. Wall Street is the main villain in the movie. He is a dangerous criminal who hires Frank Martin to transport illegal packages. He is involved in human trafficking and becomes the main enemy that Frank must fight against.
